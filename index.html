About US

Arkila is a Filipino-made app for safe, hassle-free deliveries and ordering your needs online without the need to go out.

The company was established in the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic and served as a source of income to Filipinos who lost their jobs.

Arkila was intentionally made to connect people through an extra hand in delivering items door-to-door.
